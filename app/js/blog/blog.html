Alpine Ready

Adam Stansfield
13 Sept 2015

For our first blog, we’re focusing on an instructor—Adam Stansfield. Adam works at the Alpine Shop, a local outdoor recreational store in Columbia, Missouri. He’s hiked and backpacked throughout his life, and he’s super passionate about outdoor recreation. He teaches people how to backpack and hike, and he knows just about everything there is to know about backpacks, tents, sleeping bags, and more.
